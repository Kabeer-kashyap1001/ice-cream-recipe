coffee and chocolate ice cream(recipe)
ingredients-
amul cream - 2 1/2 cups(put in fridge before whipping )
milk maid - 1/4 cup 
coffee powder- 3 teaspoon 
any chocolate slap - (amul/cadburry)50 dram melted 

beat the cream till it starts thickening now add melted chocolate, coffee powder,
milk maid and continue beating till it is a dropping consistency 
put in box and then freeze in an alluminium box and freeze over night and enjoy.
